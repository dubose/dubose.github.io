Who I Am

As my site name suggests, I like to design things. I crave purposeful creation. Most of my childhood hobbies allowed for artistic license, from Legos to musical performance to soccer, and these things showed me the joy of taking something that exists in my mind and making it exist in the world. But more than brute creation, I want the things I do to matter. I want the engineering problems I tackle to affect people. I want my art to be relateable. I want my dancing to be inspiring and intriguing to watch. To me, making something that has no effect on others is hardly worth making.

Cuando vees español en mi sitio, sepa que lo he escrito yo. Quizás usara la Red para ayudarme, pero no he pedido la asistencia de ningún otro persona. Seguro que hay errores (ojalá muy poco), pero quiero darles a los visitantes una imagen genuina de mi habilidad.
